General Objective

To be able to raise an environmental awareness to the society through the use of the proposed web application that would allow users to take a screenshot of the environmental concern.

Research Motivation

Global warming is becoming worse in the past few years, Philippines is the third biggest contributor to ocean pollution-worldwide. Some areas in the Philippines mainly focus on blooming business or industry, the environmental issues have been put aside and it is becoming worse than ever. This motivates the researchers to conduct crowdsourcing on environmental issues and how long the environmental issue has not been solved. Once the crowdsourcing is done, the researchers will talk to LGU that these issues need to be solved right away.

Improving Nature

This study will be undertaken to find out the environmental issues facing in communities and to identify how these environmental problems affect the communities
